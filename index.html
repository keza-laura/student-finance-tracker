About

The Student Finance Tracker is a simple tool designed to help students manage their expenses and keep track of their monthly spending. You can:

  • Add and edit your financial transactions easily.
  • Monitor your total spending and set monthly limits.
  • Filter and sort your records to analyze your habits.
  • Track your spending trends with a dashboard summary.

Built with simplicity in mind, this tool helps students stay organized and develop good financial habits early on.
